ABOUT THE CASTLE

A Timeless Fortress

Built in 1477 AD by Sultan AL-Ashraf Qaitbay on the
ruins of the Ancient LightHouse of Alexandria , the castle
was cconstructed to defend the city from invasions
and to stand as a symbol of strength and protection.
